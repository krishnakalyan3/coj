Family special Summer camp

A week long residential summer camp in the lap of nature, Black Forest. With a focus on yoga, meditation, and deep relaxation techniques, the summer camp helps kids and teens develop their strength, stamina, perseverance and overall personality.

Additionally, the Summer Camp provides an experience to challenge your body, overcome fear, reconnect with nature, nurture the valour within, socialise, relax and have fun. With special workshops, nature walks, trails, hikes, dancing and much more, the summer camp promises to be fun unlimited.

Frequently Asked Questions

What languages are spoken in the camp?

The main language in all our camps is English. As the camp is taking place in Germany, all announcements and explanations are here therefore always in German and English. Of course, all our trainers with their different nationalities are also available for individual translations.

Are boys and girls separated?

Girls and boys are accommodated in separate rooms. The program is, however, completely mixed.

Drugs, alcohol & Camp?

We follow a No Drug, No Alcohol policy. We strongly believe that drugs of any kind do not belong in the hands of children & teenagers, any possession or consumption of drugs is forbidden for teenagers and children in the camp and also outside the camp. The same rule applies for accompanying adults, if any.

Violations can lead to exclusion or even to criminal charges. The term "drugs" also includes cigarettes and alcohol! Through our varied activities, we offer a much better alternative! We would like to make it clear from the outset that we are also against any form of discrimination or "putting down". This is - just like violence - immediately prevented by us, in order to offer each young person a relaxed and joyful time in the camp.

How is the food at the Camp?

Full board for the entire duration of the camp is of course already included in the camp price. In addition, water and fruit are available for the participants around the clock. In case of special allergies or intolerances of your children let us know in advance and we will try to find a solution.

Bad Antogast kitchen is a proud vegetarian, vegan kitchen.

Transfers & shuttles?

Yes, we organise transfers to & from the nearest train station, Oppenau. Do reach out to us at info@artofliving.de after you have registered for the camp and we will help you organise.

Can my 12 yr old come with her friends for the camp or is it compulsorily a family camp?

Yes, children above 12 yr can attend the camp without their parents. They can arrive with their friends or alone.

Electronic equipment and valuables?

We recommend that you do not take electronic equipment and valuables with you. We do not assume any liability. We are of the opinion that the camp time is a special experience for the participants if they do not have the headphones in their ears all the time or are busy with their mobile phones. Instead they have the chance to deal with other topics and they find time to dedicate themselves to the new people in the camp.
